Collaborations

During the time period of Beethoven's life, collaborations with other artists were not commonly practiced. Beethoven did have a couple of "collaborations" with other artists though. They were:

  • A publisher by the name of Anton Diabelli invited a large number of Austrian composers to write variations to the Walz. Beethoven not only accepted (along with 51 other composers), but he wrote 33 variations that were published as his Diabelli Variations, Op 120.
  • The second considered "collaboration" would be considered more of a transformation by antoher artist. In this specific case, Franz Liszt, as well as many others, have taken Beethoven's work and arranged them to use other combinations of instruments.

Aliases

Beethoven's only known alias is "Maestro", but is assumed to have others.
